What is this?

This is a super low-tech collection of my personal recipes. I usually put a recipe on here once a friend has asked me about it.
Since this is a new version, I will try to grow it with more recipes, imported from the old version…
Additionally, I will try to find a nice way to add images to the recipes. This way you can see what the result should look like, and also get some in-the-making pictures of the steps for better reproducability.
