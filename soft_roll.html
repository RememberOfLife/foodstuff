Soft Roll

Foodstuffs Basics
Cake Batter Part 1 (per roll)
Egg whites 4 pcs.
Sugar 50 g
Cake Batter Part 2 (per roll)
Egg yolks 4 pcs.
Sugar 100 g
Vegetable Oil 55 g
Salt 1.5 g
Vanilla Flavor some
Fine Flour 60 g
Cocoa Powder 25 g
Baking Powder 1/2 tsp.
Filling (per roll)
Whipping Cream 200 g
Powdered Sugar 25 g
Vanilla Flavor some
Topping (per roll)
Whipping Cream 50 g
Chocolate Chips 100 g
1
Pre-heat oven to 180Â°C, with or without convection.
2
Prepare sheet pan with e.g. 27 x 33 cm.
Oil the bottom. Put parchment paper. Oil the parchment paper.
3
Separate 4 large eggs for the two batter parts.
4
Batter part 1: Add sugar to egg whites. Mix until stiff peaks.
5
Batter part 2: Add sugar, oil, salt, vanilla flavor. Mix until smooth.
Sift the dry ingredients, to avoid clumps.
If you want to, you can just not use cocoa powder.
6
Batter part 2: Add pre-mixed dry flour and baking poweder and cocoa powder. Mix until smooth.
6
Fold the batter part 2 into the batter part 1 in 3 batches, just enough, until no more white streaks are visible. Don't overmix it.
7
Spread the mix very evenly on the sheet pan. Bake it for 9 to 12 minutes, just enough until springy firm.
